Design v Manufacture Locations

Click the button below to switch from design to manufacture geovisualisation.

  

A key takeaway from the label data set is the clustered distribution of design headquarters versus manufacture. From the 34 label data set, we can see that design is majority clustered in Europe whereas manufacture has a large cluster in South East Asia. This centralised design versus decentralised manufacture could be an insight into the opportunites offered to design graduates in each country. This information could also be used if a consumer is trying to buy carbon neutral clothing as brands that design and produce in close proximidy produce less carbon.
